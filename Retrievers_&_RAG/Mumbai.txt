Tourist Spot: Gateway of India, Marine Drive, Elephanta Caves, Bandra-Worli Sea Link etc.
Restaurant: Bademiya (for kebabs), Leopold Café (iconic), The Bombay Canteen (modern), Ram Ashraya for South Indian breakfast etc. 


Dishes & Specialties: Vada Pav, Pav Bhaji, Mumbai style chaats (Sev Puri, Bhel Puri, Pani Puri), seafood etc. Street-food is huge here. 


Chef: Chef Gaggan Anand is associated with the high-end restaurant.
